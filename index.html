My Research

I research the underlying cognitive and neural mechanisms of cognitive control. How does control emerge from the brain? How does the brain accomplish superficially easy tasks? I am currently interested in answering these questions looking at the interaction between bottom-up and top-down control processes in working and episodic memory.
